Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is this website for?

This is the MemeMaster web application! It can be used to view, collect, and even share awesome memes with other meme enthusiasts like yourself!

Question 2: How do you upload a meme?

We make uploading memes super easy! Simply go to the "Uploads" page from the nav bar and then either browse your local file system for the meme you wish to upload or enter in the URL of where the meme is located.

Question 3: How can I create my own meme?

To create a meme of your own, all you need to do is go to the "Create" page from the nav bar and then add the picture for the meme, as well as some text to the top and/or bottom and then click "Upload a meme". Simple as that.
